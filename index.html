Overview

The three live-action Spider-Men each bring a unique take to Peter Parker. Tobey Maguire brings emotional depth and responsibility, Andrew Garfield adds charm and intensity, and Tom Holland blends youth, vulnerability, and MCU integration.

Tobey Maguire

Origin story and classic trilogy portrayal focused on responsibility.

Andrew Garfield

More emotional, modern, fast-moving portrayal with Gwen Stacy's storyline.

Tom Holland

MCU-connected, young, energetic portrayal with Tony Stark mentorship.
